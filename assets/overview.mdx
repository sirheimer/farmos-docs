## What Are Assets?

Assets are any files you upload to Juice:
- **Videos** - MP4, MOV, AVI
- **Images** - JPG, PNG, GIF
- **Audio** - MP3, WAV
- **Files** - PDF, docs, spreadsheets

**Use assets for:**
- Reference images in content generation
- Source material for editing
- Brand asset storage
- Content library organization

## Accessing Assets

Click **Assets** in the sidebar to view your library.

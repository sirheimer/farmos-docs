## Images

**Formats:** JPG, JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP, SVG  
**Max size:** 10 MB  
**Recommended:** PNG for logos (transparency), JPG for photos

**Use for:**
- Product photography
- Brand graphics
- Reference images
- Screenshots

## Videos

**Formats:** MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, WebM  
**Max size:** 500 MB  
**Recommended:** MP4 (H.264) for best compatibility

**Use for:**
- Source footage storage
- Backup of posted videos
- Raw content library

## Audio

**Formats:** MP3, WAV, AAC, M4A, FLAC  
**Max size:** 50 MB  
**Recommended:** MP3 for music, WAV for high-quality

**Use for:**
- Background music (future feature)
- Audio clips
- Voiceovers

## Files

**Formats:** PDF, DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX, TXT, CSV  
**Max size:** 25 MB  

**Use for:**
- Brand guidelines
- Content calendars
- Reference documents

## Optimization

**Automatic:**
- Images: Resized to web-optimized versions
- Videos: Compressed for faster loading
- Thumbnails: Generated automatically

**Original files preserved** for download.

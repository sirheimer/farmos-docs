## How to Upload

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Assets Page">
    Click **Assets** in sidebar
  </Step>

  <Step title="Drag and Drop">
    Drag files from your computer onto the page
  </Step>

  <Step title="Or Click Upload Button">
    Browse and select files
  </Step>

  <Step title="Files Process">
    Upload progress shown
  </Step>

  <Step title="Assets Available">
    Files appear in your library
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported File Types

**Images:** JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, SVG  
**Videos:** MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV  
**Audio:** MP3, WAV, AAC, M4A  
**Files:** PDF, DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX, TXT  

## File Size Limits

**Per file:**
- Images: 10 MB max
- Videos: 500 MB max
- Audio: 50 MB max
- Files: 25 MB max

## Bulk Uploads

Upload multiple files at once:
- Select up to 50 files
- Drag entire folders
- Automatic batching for large uploads

## Creating Folders

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Create Folder'">
    From Assets page
  </Step>

  <Step title="Name Your Folder">
    E.g., "Product Photos Q1 2026"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create">
    Folder appears in library
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Moving Assets

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Assets">
    Check boxes next to files
  </Step>

  <Step title="Drag to Folder">
    Drag selected assets onto folder
  </Step>

  <Step title="Files Moved">
    Assets now inside folder
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Selection Criteria

Choose templates based on:

1. **Your content type** - What you want to talk about
2. **Your audience** - Who you're targeting
3. **Your goal** - Awareness vs leads vs sales
4. **Your testing capacity** - How many you can manage

## Template Selection Framework

### Then Add Specialized Templates

After mastering the big 3, add templates for specific use cases:

**For thought leadership:**
- "Common Mistakes..."
- "The Truth About..."
- "What Nobody Tells You..."

**For product marketing:**
- "Before & After"
- "Feature Showcase"
- "Product Comparison"

**For storytelling:**
- "Our Journey"
- "Customer Story"
- "Behind the Scenes"

## Templates by Industry

### B2B SaaS

**Best performers:**
1. "3 Ways to [Solve Problem]"
2. "How to [Achieve Outcome] Without [Pain Point]"
3. "[X] Mistakes [Role] Make with [Topic]"

**Example topics:**
- "3 ways to reduce customer churn without discounts"
- "How to automate reporting without hiring a data team"
- "5 mistakes founders make with their first SaaS pricing"

### E-Commerce/DTC

**Best performers:**
1. "Why [Product] is Different"
2. "[X] Ways to Use [Product]"
3. "Before & After Using [Product]"

**Example topics:**
- "Why our water bottle keeps drinks cold for 48 hours"
- "5 ways to style this jacket for different occasions"
- "My skin before and after using this serum for 30 days"

### Services/Consulting

**Best performers:**
1. "[X] Signs You Need [Service]"
2. "How We [Achieved Result] for [Client Type]"
3. "The [Process] That [Outcome]"

**Example topics:**
- "5 signs your company needs a fractional CFO"
- "How we 3x'd revenue for a B2B SaaS in 6 months"
- "The sales process that closes 60% of enterprise deals"

## Adding/Removing Templates

### Adding Templates

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Templates Page">
    Click Templates in sidebar
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse Unselected Templates">
    See templates not in your library
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click 'Add to Library'">
    Template is now available in content generator
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Removing Templates

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Templates Page">
    Your selected templates show with checkmarks
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click 'Remove'">
    Template removed from your library
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Removing a template doesn't affect already-generated content using that template.
</Info>

## Template Limits

**No limit on selected templates** - add as many as you want!

**Recommended:**
- Beginners: 3-5 templates
- Intermediate: 5-10 templates
- Advanced: 10-15 templates

**Why limit yourself:** Easier to master a few templates than spread thin across many.

## Listicle Templates

**Format:** "[Number] Ways/Tips/Secrets/etc."

**Structure:**
- Hook/Title slide
- Item #1 with visual
- Item #2 with visual
- Item #3 with visual
- (continued...)
- CTA slide

**Variations:**
- 3 Ways Template
- 5 Tips Template
- 7 Secrets Template
- 10 Hacks Template

**Best for:** Quick value delivery, easy consumption

**Example topics:**
- "3 ways to improve landing page conversion"
- "5 tools every marketer needs in 2026"
- "7 signs you're ready to scale your startup"

## How-To/Tutorial Templates

**Format:** Step-by-step instructional content

**Structure:**
- Problem statement
- Step 1
- Step 2
- Step 3
- Final result
- CTA

**Best for:** Educational content, skill-building

**Example topics:**
- "How to write cold emails that get responses"
- "How to set up Google Ads in 10 minutes"
- "How to hire your first engineer"

## Problem/Solution Templates

**Format:** Present problem â†’ Introduce solution

**Structure:**
- Hook (state problem)
- Amplify pain
- Introduce solution (your product)
- Show how it works
- Results/benefits
- CTA

**Best for:** Product marketing, converting awareness to interest

**Example topics:**
- "Remote teams waste 10 hours in meetings - here's the fix"
- "Your CRM is costing you deals - we solved it"

## Before/After Templates

**Format:** Show transformation

**Structure:**
- "Before" state
- Pain points of before
- What changed
- "After" state
- Benefits of after
- How to get results

**Best for:** Demonstrating value, showing results

**Example topics:**
- "Our checkout flow before vs after optimization"
- "My productivity before vs after time blocking"

## Social Proof Templates

**Format:** Showcase results, testimonials, case studies

**Structure:**
- Client/customer intro
- Their problem
- Your solution
- Specific results
- Key takeaway

**Best for:** Building credibility, converting prospects

**Example topics:**
- "How we helped [Client] 3x their revenue in 6 months"
- "This customer was skeptical - then hit $100K MRR"

## Thought Leadership Templates

**Format:** Take a stance, share insights

**Structure:**
- Contrarian hook
- Common belief
- Why it's wrong
- Your perspective
- Supporting evidence
- CTA

**Best for:** Building authority, attracting attention

**Example topics:**
- "Most SaaS pricing is wrong - here's why"
- "Stop following Instagram gurus - here's what actually works"

## Choosing By Slide Count

Templates vary in length:

| Slide Count | Ideal For | Watch Time |
|-------------|-----------|------------|
| 4-5 slides | Quick tips, fast hooks | 8-12 seconds |
| 6-8 slides | Most content (sweet spot) | 15-20 seconds |
| 9-12 slides | In-depth, complex topics | 25-35 seconds |

**Recommendation:** Start with 6-8 slide templates (best retention)

## Template Anatomy

Every template has:

**Visual elements:**
- Background styling
- Image placeholders
- Text containers
- Logo placement (branded accounts)

**Text elements:**
- Headline formats
- Body copy styling
- CTA positioning

**Animation:**
- Slide transitions
- Text animations
- Image reveals

You can edit all of these during content creation!

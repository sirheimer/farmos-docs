## What is a Template?

A template defines the "format" of your video:
- Number of slides (typically 6-10)
- Text layout and positioning
- Image placement and sizing
- Animation style and transitions
- Overall visual structure

**Think of templates like PowerPoint templates** - the design framework that you fill with your content.

## Why Templates Matter

Templates are optimized for viewer retention:
- Proven slide progressions that hold attention
- Text placement that doesn't get cut off
- Timing that matches average watch time
- Hooks designed for maximum impact

**Data-backed:** Each template is based on analysis of viral TikToks in its category.

## Browse Templates

Access templates from:
- **Templates page** in sidebar
- **Onboarding flow** (select initial templates)
- **Content generator** (choose template when creating)

### Template Library Size

- Problem/Solution formats
- Listicles (3 ways, 5 tips, etc.)
- How-to guides
- Before/After showcases
- Storytelling formats
- Educational deep-dives

**New templates added monthly** based on trending formats.

## Template Performance

Not all templates perform equally for all brands. Test and optimize:

**After 14 days, review:**
- Which templates get highest average views?
- Which get best engagement?
- Which drive the most followers?

**Then:**
- Create 80% of content with top-performing templates
- Keep 20% for testing new templates

## Template Best Practices

**Start with proven templates:**
- "3 Ways to..." (highest success rate)
- "Problem & Solution"
- "How to... in X Steps"

**Test before committing:**
- Create 3-5 pieces with a template before judging
- Give it 14 days to see performance
- Compare to other templates

**Match to content type:**
- Product features → Feature showcase templates
- Educational → How-to or listicle templates
- Brand story → Storytelling templates

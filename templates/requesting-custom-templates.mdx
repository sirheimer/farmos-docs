## When to Request Custom Templates

**Request custom templates when:**
- Your industry has unique visual styles not covered by existing templates
- You have a specific viral format you want to replicate
- Your brand has strict design requirements
- You've exhausted existing templates and need fresh formats

**Use existing templates when:**
- You're just starting (first 30 days)
- Existing templates are performing well
- You haven't tested all templates yet

<Tip>
**Test existing templates first.** Most brands find 2-3 templates that work well in the existing library before needing custom ones.
</Tip>

    - Contact email

  <Step title="Submit Request">

  </Step>
</Steps>

### Reference Videos

**Provide 2-4 TikTok URLs** showing the style you want:
- Link to viral TikToks using similar format
- Videos from your niche
- Competitors' content (for format only)

**Example:**
```
Reference 1: https://tiktok.com/@brand/video/123 
(Love the slide transition style)

Reference 2: https://tiktok.com/@competitor/video/456
(Similar layout to what we need)
```

### Use Case Description

**Explain how you'll use it:**
- "We'll create 10+ pieces of content showcasing our different features"
- "This will be our primary template for educational content"
- "We need this for product launch campaigns"

**Why it matters:** Helps team prioritize and understand impact.

### Unique Requirements

**Mention any specific needs:**
- Must show side-by-side comparisons
- Needs space for long-form text
- Requires specific aspect ratios
- Must accommodate video clips (not just images)

## Request Evaluation

Templates are evaluated on:

**Feasibility:**
- Can it be built with current tech?
- Does it work within TikTok constraints?
- Is it too similar to existing templates?

**Value:**
- Would other customers use it?
- Is it a unique use case?
- Does it fit Juice's content philosophy?

**Effort:**
- How complex is the build?
- How long to create and test?
- Does it require new features?

## Custom Template Pricing

**Included in plan:**
- 2 custom template requests per year (Pro plan)
- 5 custom template requests per year (Enterprise plan)

**Additional requests:**
- $500 per custom template
- Or contact sales for template packs

## Alternative: Template Modification Requests

**Instead of full custom template:**

Request modifications to existing templates:
- "Can you make the 3 Ways template with 5 items instead?"
- "Can you add logo watermark to all slides in Problem/Solution template?"

**These are faster** (2-3 days) and often free.

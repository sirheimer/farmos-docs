## What is a Template?

A template defines:
- **Number of slides** (usually 6-10)
- **Text placement** and styling
- **Image layout** and positioning
- **Animation style** and transitions
- **Overall visual aesthetic**

**Example:** The "3 Ways Template" has:
- Slide 1: Hook + title
- Slide 2: Way #1 with image
- Slide 3: Way #2 with image
- Slide 4: Way #3 with image
- Slide 5: CTA

## How to Select a Template

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Content Generator">
    Click **Content** in the sidebar, or click **+ Create Content** button.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse Templates">
    You'll see your selected templates (chosen during onboarding). Each template shows:
    - Template name
    - Preview thumbnail
    - Slide count
    - Category
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click a Template">
    Click to select. The template card will highlight, and you'll automatically move to the next step (choosing a topic).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
**Don't see many templates?** You can add more templates anytime from the [Templates page](/templates/overview).
</Info>

## Choosing the Right Template

Different content goals need different templates:

### For Educational Content

**Best templates:**
- "3 Ways to..." - Listicle format
- "How to..." - Step-by-step guide
- "Complete Guide to..." - In-depth tutorial

**Use when:** Teaching your audience something actionable

### For Product Content

**Best templates:**
- "Problem & Solution" - Pain point → Your product
- "Before & After" - Life without vs with your product
- "Feature Showcase" - Highlight specific features

**Use when:** Driving product awareness or conversions

### For Thought Leadership

**Best templates:**
- "Common Mistakes" - Industry insights
- "The Truth About..." - Contrarian take
- "Story" templates - Founder journey, origin story

**Use when:** Building authority and brand awareness

## Template Preview

Before selecting, you can preview templates:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Hover Over Template">
    Hover over any template card to see a preview
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click to Expand">
    Click the template to see full details including:
    - All slide layouts
    - Animation style
    - Example topics
    - Ideal use cases
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Switching Templates Mid-Flow

You can change templates anytime:

1. Click **← Back** to return to template selection
2. Select a different template
3. Your topic and settings carry over
4. Re-generate content with new template

<Warning>
Switching templates after generating content will discard your current slides. Make sure to export or save any content you want to keep.
</Warning>

## Managing Your Template Library

From the **Templates** page, you can:
- Add new templates to your library
- Remove templates you don't use
- Preview template examples
- Request custom templates

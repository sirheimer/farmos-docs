## Viewing Posts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Posts' in Sidebar">
    Opens posts page showing all posted content
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse Grid View">
    See thumbnails of all videos with:
    - View count
    - Engagement stats
    - Posted date
    - Account name
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Post for Details">
    See full analytics and options
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Filtering Posts

**Filter by:**
- **Account** - See posts from specific accounts
- **Date range** - Last 7 days, 30 days, all time
- **Performance** - High views, viral posts, low performers
- **Status** - Posted, scheduled, failed

## Downloading Posts

Download individual videos or bulk export:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Posts">
    Check boxes next to posts you want
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click 'Download'">
    Downloads MP4 files
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Use for:** Sharing on other platforms, client reports, portfolio

## Post Analytics

Each post shows:
- Views, likes, comments, shares
- Engagement rate
- Watch time
- Viral status (if 100K+ views)

## Core Metrics

### Views

**What it is:** Number of times your videos were watched  
**Why it matters:** Primary reach metric  
**Good benchmark:** 10K+ per post

### Likes

**What it is:** Hearts/likes from viewers  
**Why it matters:** Engagement signal to algorithm  
**Good benchmark:** 3-5% of views

### Comments

**What it is:** Viewer comments on posts  
**Why it matters:** Strong engagement signal  
**Good benchmark:** 0.5-1% of views

### Shares

**What it is:** Times video was shared  
**Why it matters:** Highest-value engagement (amplifies reach)  
**Good benchmark:** 1-2% of views

### Engagement Rate

**Formula:** (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Views × 100  
**Good benchmark:** 5-8%

### Viral Rate

**Formula:** Viral posts / Total posts × 100  
**Good benchmark:** 5-10%

## Metric Priorities

**Most important to least:**

1. **Views** - Reach is foundation
2. **Engagement rate** - Quality of attention
3. **Viral rate** - Exceptional content %
4. **Followers** - Least important (algorithm doesn't favor followers)

<Info>
**Focus on views first.** Everything else follows from reach.
</Info>

## What's a "Good" Number?

### By Account Age

| Metric | Week 1 | Week 4 | Week 12 |
|--------|--------|--------|---------|
| Avg views | 1-3K | 5-10K | 10-25K+ |
| Engagement | 2-4% | 4-6% | 5-8% |
| Viral posts | 0-1 | 1-2 | 3-5 |

### By Industry

**B2B SaaS:** Lower views, higher engagement  
**E-commerce:** Higher views, moderate engagement  
**Creator/Agency:** Variable, trend-dependent

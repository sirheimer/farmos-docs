## Accessing the Dashboard

Click **Overview** in the sidebar (or it's your landing page after login).

## Key Metrics

### Top Stats Cards

**Total Accounts** - All accounts created  
**Accounts Posting** - Currently active accounts  
**Posts (timeframe)** - Posts in selected time range  
**Total Views** - Cumulative views across all content  
**Total Likes** - Cumulative engagement  
**Total Comments** - Conversation generated  
**Total Shares** - Viral distribution

Each stat shows:
- Current value
- % change from previous period
- Trend indicator (↑ or ↓)
- Sparkline chart

## Views Chart

Interactive chart showing performance over time:

**Controls:**
- **Time range:** Today, 7 days, 30 days, all time
- **Timeframe:** Hourly, daily, weekly
- **Metric:** Views, likes, comments, shares, posts
- **Mode:** Cumulative or incremental

### Reading the Chart

- **X-axis:** Time period
- **Y-axis:** Count (views, likes, etc.)
- **Line:** Performance trend
- **Blue indicator:** Current time (for "Today" view)

**Hover any point** to see exact numbers.

## Top Performing Content

Grid of your best posts sorted by views:

- Click time range to filter
- See thumbnails + view counts
- Click to view full analytics

**Use to:** Identify winning content to duplicate.

## Top Accounts

Table showing your highest-performing accounts:

**Metrics shown:**
- Views
- Likes
- Posts count
- Status

**Sorted by:** Total views (highest first)

## Viral Milestones

Track content that reaches viral thresholds:

**Bronze:** 100K+ views  
**Silver:** 500K+ views  
**Gold:** 1M+ views  
**Diamond:** 10M+ views  

See count of posts at each level and your overall viral rate %.

[→ Learn about viral milestones](/analytics/viral-milestones)

## Time Range Selection

Choose time period for all metrics:

- **Today** - Last 24 hours
- **Yesterday** - Previous 24 hours
- **Last 3 days**
- **Last 7 days**
- **Last 30 days**
- **All time**

**All dashboard metrics update** when you change time range.

## Live Updates

Dashboard shows **Live** badge - metrics update in real-time as:
- New posts go live
- Views increase
- Accounts start posting
- Content performs

**Auto-refresh:** Every 30 seconds (or manual refresh)

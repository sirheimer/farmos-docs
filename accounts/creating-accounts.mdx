## Account Types: Which to Choose?

Choose your account mix based on goals:

<Tabs>

    **What they are:**
    - Use your company name in username
    - Display your logo as profile picture
    - Bio states "Official [YourBrand] account"

    - Professional

    - Brand-focused

    - Product features and demos

    - Industry insights
    - Customer success stories

## Recommended Account Mixes

Choose based on your goals:

### For B2B SaaS (6 accounts)
- 4 Branded accounts (thought leadership, features)
- 2 Faceless accounts (educational content)

### For DTC E-commerce (6 accounts)
- 2 Branded accounts (product showcases)
- 2 Faceless accounts (how-to's, tips)
- 2 UGC accounts (reviews, testimonials)

### For Agencies/Content Creators (6 accounts)
- 3 Faceless accounts (niche content)
- 3 UGC accounts (personal perspective)

### For Maximum Testing (12 accounts)
- 4 of each type to see what performs best

  <Step title="Choose Account Mix">

  <Step title="Set Preferences">
    - **Tone of Voice:** Casual, Professional, Friendly, etc.
    - **Gender Preference:** Male, Female, Neutral (for UGC accounts)

  <Step title="Click 'Generate Profiles'">
    AI creates unique profiles for each account:
    - Usernames

    - Profile pictures

  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Generated Profiles">
    See preview of all accounts. You can:
    - Edit usernames
    - Regenerate profile pictures
    - Customize bios
    - Adjust keywords/hashtags
  </Step>

  <Step title="Finalize and Create">
    Click **Create Accounts** to confirm
  </Step>

  <Step title="Automation Begins">
    Juice automatically:
    - Creates accounts on TikTok
    - Sets up profiles
    - Begins warmup process
    
    **No phone management needed**
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Total time:** ~5 minutes (then automation takes over)

## Customizing Generated Profiles

Before confirming account creation, you can customize each profile:

### Editing Usernames

**Click username to edit:**
- Keep it short (12-20 characters)
- Make it memorable
- Include niche keywords
- Avoid numbers if possible

**Examples:**

**AI generated:** `productivitytips47`  
**Better:** `productivitylab`

**AI generated:** `yourcompanyofficial1`  
**Better:** `yourcompany`

## Account Creation Settings

### Tone of Voice

Choose the overall tone for generated bios and content targeting:

- **Casual** - "Hey, here's a cool tip..."
- **Professional** - "Research shows that..."
- **Friendly** - "Let me help you with..."
- **Authoritative** - "Based on 10 years of data..."

**Default:** Uses tone from your Brand Kit

### Gender Preference (UGC Only)

For person-based accounts, choose:
- **Male** - AI generates male personas
- **Female** - AI generates female personas
- **Neutral** - Mix of both

**Recommendation:** Use "Neutral" to test what resonates with your audience.

## What Happens After Creation?

Once you click "Create Accounts," automation begins:

### Immediate (Minutes)

- Accounts appear in your Accounts list
- Status: **Pending** â†’ **Setting Up**
- You can assign content even during setup

### Within 1-2 Hours

- Accounts created on TikTok

- Warmup day counter starts

### After 3-7 Days

- Warmup completes
- Status changes to: **Posting**
- Assigned content begins posting automatically

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I create accounts without content ready?">
    Yes! Create accounts first, assign content later. Accounts will warmup while you create content.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long until accounts start posting?">
    3-7 days for warmup, then posting begins immediately. Total time from creation to first post: ~7 days.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I edit account profiles after creation?">
    Yes, but only before posting starts. Once an account is posting, username/bio changes aren't recommended (can affect algorithm).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I run out of account slots?">
    Upgrade your plan to get more slots, or archive underperforming accounts to free up space.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I manually post to these accounts?">
    No. Juice-managed accounts are fully automated. For manual posting, use your personal TikTok account.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Best Practices

### Start Small

**First batch:** 4-6 accounts  
**Why:** Easier to manage, faster to learn, less overwhelming

**After 30 days:** Add more based on performance.

### Mix Account Types

**Don't:** Create 6 of the same type  
**Do:** Mix 3-4 types to test what works

**Why:** Different types reach different audiences.

### Batch Creation

**Don't:** Create 1 account at a time over weeks  
**Do:** Create all accounts in one batch

**Why:** They warmup together, start posting together, easier to compare performance.

### Custom Profiles Matter

**Spend 5 minutes customizing profiles:**
- Better usernames = higher follow rates
- Better bios = clearer value proposition
- Better profile pics = more professional appearance
